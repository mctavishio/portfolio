definitions

transmedia
"Henry Jenkins definition of transmedia: the art of telling one story over multiple media, where each medium is making a unique contribution to the whole."
My personal definition od trans/media integrates both digital and analogue forms. I think of it as cross-platform storytelling that creatively explores place, time, is non-linear and multithreaded, has multiple access points, often facilitates active participation by audience / viewers and thoughtfully and expansively uses cross-media tools to engage, weave and explore story.

creative commons
from Wiki: Creative Commons has been described as being at the forefront of the copyleft movement, which seeks to support the building of a richer public domain by providing an alternative to the automatic "all rights reserved" copyright, and has been dubbed "some rights reserved."[10] David Berry and Giles Moss have credited Creative Commons with generating interest in the issue of intellectual property and contributing to the re-thinking of the role of the "commons" in the "information age".
semantic web
"The Semantic Web is a collaborative movement led by international standards body the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C).[1] The standard promotes common data formats on the World Wide Web. By encouraging the inclusion of semantic content in web pages, the Semantic Web aims at converting the current web, dominated by unstructured and semi-structured documents into a 'web of data'."

^ about

::: b r 1 N k ::: was an episode in the larger 2015-2016 railway prophecies series funded by the the jerome foundation .

it was created in part for the c. p. snow lecture series @ ithaca college. here it was used as an immersive lecture environment & an interactive one-day gallery installation / sharing session (april 14-15, 2016).

::: b r 1 N k ::: was additionally the "score" / immersive enclosure for a sound lab with zeitgeist new music ensemble at the food farm's new community root cellar in wrenshall, mn (march 19, 2016).

this work was shared in an interactive installation / testing / technology deep-dive session at duluth's web technology meetup in (february, 25 2016) and as a work-in progress discussion with artists / community members at the duluth art institute (january 7, 2016).

::: b r 1 N k ::: was a networked, interactive machine symphony built to work on both a mobile & desktop. it had a simple mobile response to "touch" & a cross-screen type / telegraph aspect that operated with or wothout a human typist.
